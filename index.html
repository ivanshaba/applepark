Frequently Asked Questions

What is IPTV?

IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) is a digital television service that is delivered over the internet instead of traditional cable or satellite.

How does IPTV work?

IPTV uses internet protocol to deliver TV channels and content to your device. It requires a stable internet connection and a compatible device such as a smart TV, set-top box, or streaming device.
